Self-Reflection Week 4

发表于2025-10-12|更新于2025-10-12|英文SR
|总字数:203|阅读时长:1分钟|浏览量:

You have now experienced your first several weeks of university. This included lectures, labs, seminars, tutorials, tasks, discussions, homework, etc.

Many of those required a large amount of English.

Think about everything you experienced so far and brainstorm some problems and challenges you have met, especially ones that involved or required English.

Write down a sentence to describe two clear challenges or problems. One sentence for each that explains, in general, what the problem is. Then, for each problem, write freely (no word or sentence limits) about why you feel it is important to solve that problem so that it (or similar problems) doesn’t happen again.

The first challenge is that the English courses require us to write long essays that I was a little bit confused about how to organize the whole passage at first. By solving this problem, my writing ability will improve. Also, it will help me to write my paper better in the future.

The second challenge is the discussion in English with classmates. In both lectures and discussion classes, when I discuss with my classmates, I often encounter word problems, which affect my fluency very much. By, solving this problem, I can communicate and express myself better.
